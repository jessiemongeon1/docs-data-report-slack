Notable Takeaways

Spam consumes more than one-quarter of all chatbot capacity

55 of 208 Kapa conversations (26.4%) are spam or off-topic, many containing explicit adult content strings.

A topic guard could reclaim over a quarter of chatbot compute and improve response quality metrics for legitimate users.

Walrus Sites tutorial achieves perfect engagement despite minimal traffic

46 combined visitors across /walrus-sites/tutorial and /walrus-sites/tutorial.html recorded 0–9% bounce rate and 100% scroll depth.

The tutorial format works exceptionally well; replicating it for blob storage and CLI setup could lift engagement on high-traffic low-scroll pages.

ChatGPT referrals spend dramatically more time on docs than any other source

12 ChatGPT-referred visitors averaged 329 seconds on site versus 38 seconds for Google and 38 seconds for direct traffic.

AI-assisted discovery is producing the highest-quality sessions, warranting structured support for AI citation of Walrus docs.

Operator pages show near-zero bounce despite niche audience size

/docs/operator-guide recorded 8% bounce rate and /docs/operator-guide/storage-nodes recorded 7% bounce rate among 118 and 44 visitors respectively.

Operator documentation is highly effective at retaining its audience, suggesting investment in deeper operator content would yield strong returns.
