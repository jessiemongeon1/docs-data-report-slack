Traffic Overview

docs.wal.app had 12,036 visitors and 19,569 pageviews over the last 7 days (Apr 10–16, 2026), with a bounce rate of 81% and average session duration of 40 seconds. Traffic grew steadily throughout the week, rising from 1,222 visitors on Apr 10 to 2,129 on Apr 16—a 74% increase. The vast majority of traffic (95%) arrives via direct or unknown sources. The homepage and Walrus Sites intro pages dominate consumption, indicating strong top-of-funnel interest.

Steady daily traffic growth throughout the week

Visitors grew from 1,222 on Apr 10 to 2,129 on Apr 16 (+74%), with pageviews rising from 1,760 to 3,716 (+111%). The acceleration in pageviews outpacing visitor growth suggests returning or deeper-browsing users increasing toward week-end.

Bounce rate improving over longer time horizons

7d bounce rate is 81% vs 68% over 30d, and views/visit improves from 1.4 (7d) to 1.61 (30d). This suggests the recent traffic cohort is less engaged than historical visitors — possibly driven by new top-of-funnel arrivals from announcements or campaigns.

Duplicate legacy HTML URLs splitting Walrus Sites traffic

Both /walrus-sites/intro and /walrus-sites/intro.html each received ~2,067–2,070 visitors, and similarly /walrus-sites/tutorial and /walrus-sites/tutorial.html each got 23 visitors. These duplicates split SEO value and analytics; canonical redirects should be implemented.

ChatGPT referrals show disproportionately high engagement

chatgpt.com visitors average 329s session duration vs the site average of 40s — over 8x longer. AI-referred users arrive with specific questions and explore docs thoroughly, making this a high-quality traffic source worth monitoring as AI assistant usage grows.

Operator and advanced content earns deep scroll engagement

Pages like /docs/getting-started/advanced-setup (80% scroll), /docs/system-overview/core-concepts (70%), and /docs/operator-guide/storage-nodes (66%) attract smaller but highly engaged audiences. Investing in this content tier could improve retention for technical users.
