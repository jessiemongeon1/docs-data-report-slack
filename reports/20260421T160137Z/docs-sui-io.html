Traffic Overview

docs.sui.io recorded 4,152 unique visitors and 12,326 pageviews over the 7-day period April 14–20, 2026. Engagement is moderate with a 62% bounce rate, 2.64 views per visit, and an average visit duration of 157 seconds. Traffic is driven primarily by direct visits and Google search, with onboarding and getting-started content dominating page views. The final day (April 20) was the strongest of the week with 708 visitors and 2,292 pageviews, while the weekend (April 18–19) saw the expected dip.

Weekday vs. Weekend Traffic Split

Weekday visitors (Mon–Fri) average ~640/day while weekend days (Sat–Sun) drop to ~460/day — a ~28% weekend decline confirming a developer-centric audience. Monday April 20 spiked to 708 visitors, the week's highest, suggesting post-weekend catch-up behavior.

Onboarding Funnel Dominance

The top 10 pages are overwhelmingly onboarding and getting-started content (/getting-started/onboarding, /sui-install, /get-coins, /configure-sui-client, /get-address), indicating new developer acquisition is the primary use case driving traffic rather than reference lookups.

Duplicate URL Fragmentation in Onboarding

Multiple near-duplicate paths exist for key content (e.g., /getting-started/onboarding/sui-install with 363 visitors vs. /guides/developer/getting-started/sui-install with 143 visitors). Consolidating these via redirects could concentrate SEO value and simplify analytics.

AI Chatbot Referrals Emerging

ChatGPT (25 visitors), Claude (8 visitors), and Perplexity (1 visitor) together account for 34 referral visitors this week. Claude sessions average 541s — the longest of any source — suggesting AI agents are sending highly motivated users. This channel will likely grow.

DeepBook Ecosystem Engagement

Both deepbook.tech (94 referral visitors) and /onchain-finance/deepbookv3/deepbook (107 page visitors, 20% bounce) show strong engagement, suggesting the DeepBook DeFi protocol community is an active and growing segment of Sui docs users.

Bounce Rate Improvement This Week

The 7-day bounce rate of 62% is meaningfully better than the 14-day (65%) and 30-day (65%) averages, indicating either higher-quality traffic this week or improved content relevance. The /getting-started/onboarding hub page with a 9% bounce rate is a standout performer.

gRPC Content Gaining Traction

Two gRPC-related pages appear in top traffic: /develop/accessing-data/grpc (56 visitors, highest scroll depth at 73) and /develop/accessing-data/grpc/using-grpc (24 visitors). Combined with /concepts/data-access/grpc (51 visitors), gRPC documentation is drawing consistent specialized interest.
